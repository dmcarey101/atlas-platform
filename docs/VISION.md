#Atlas Vision
Atlas is a state government affairs intelligence platform that aggregates, normalizes, and analyzes lobbying activites across all 50 states.
## Mission
Help government affairs professionals understand who is influencing policy, where, on whose behalf and how that activity changes over time.
##Initial Product Focus
Atlas will begin as a searchable database of:
- Registered lobbyists
- Lobbying Firms
- Clients/principals
- Active registrants
- Estimated lobbying compensation
- State-level filing sources
## Core User
The initial customer is corporate, trade association or non-profit government affairs leader who needs multi-state visibility into lobbying relationships and competitor activity.
## Long-Term Vision
Atlas becomes the daily intelligence layer for state government affairs teams by combining lobbying data and relationship intelligence
